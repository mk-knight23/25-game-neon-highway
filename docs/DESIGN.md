# Design System: 08-car-racing-game

## Visual Philosophy
The design follows a **Retro-Modern Arcade** aesthetic. It combines deep interstellar backgrounds with vibrant neon vehicle accents and a glassmorphic HUD.

## Design Patterns
-   **Interstellar Backdrop**: Radial gradients and pulsing glow effects to simulate space travel.
-   **Arcade HUD**: High-contrast, bold typography with backdrop blurs for score and speed indicators.
-   **Neon Vehicles**: High-saturation colors for player and enemy cars to ensure visibility against dark road surfaces.

## Color Palette
-   **Void Base**: Slate-950 (#020617)
-   **Road Surface**: Gray-900 (#111827)
-   **Player Glow**: Indigo-500 (#6366f1)
-   **Enemy Hazards**: Amber, Emerald, Rose (Dynamic selection)

## Interaction Design
-   **Responsive Steering**: Instantaneous response to directional inputs.
-   **Dynamic HUD**: Score updates and speed-up notifications in real-time.
-   **Mobile Controls**: Large, translucent touch buttons for on-the-go gameplay.
